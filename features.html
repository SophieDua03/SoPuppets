features

replacement animation

Replacement animation is a form of stop motion. But instead of making one puppet that you put through its paces, you make interchangeable pre-sculpted elements and swap them in and out.you need to create a library of various elements that are interchangeable. One of the most common needs for this method is for lip syncing.

rigs in animation

The best tools for stop motion animators are rigs and winders. They are often used to achieve complex movements like walks, jumps or making things fly. They are very handy, you can move all their parts to achieve the desired position helping your animation needs.
